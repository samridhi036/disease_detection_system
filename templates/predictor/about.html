About GastroPredict

GastroPredict is an AI-powered disease prediction system focused on gastrointestinal and hepatology conditions.

🤖

Machine Learning Model

Uses a Random Forest classifier trained on a comprehensive symptom-disease dataset covering 25 conditions including GI diseases and hepatology (hepatitis, cirrhosis, NAFLD).

🫁

Diseases Covered

GERD, IBS, Crohn's Disease, Ulcerative Colitis, Peptic Ulcer, Gastritis, Celiac Disease, Appendicitis, Colorectal Cancer, Hepatitis A/B/C, Cirrhosis, NAFLD, and more.

📊

How It Works

Select your symptoms, and the model analyzes the symptom pattern to predict the top 3 most likely conditions along with probability scores and specialist recommendations.

⚠️

Important Disclaimer

This system is for educational and informational purposes only. It is not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider.
